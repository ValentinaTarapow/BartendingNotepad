Bartending Notepad

¿What is "Bartending Notepad"?

As bartenders we know that sometimes the process of creating new cocktails can be a little bit tedious, it gets even worse at the moment of remembering them.

That is why we created Bartending Notepad as a place where we facilitate the authorship of drinks...you can also use it as a memory helper, we will not tell anyone about it.

You will be able to select the type of cocktail, its ingredients, measurements and name your recipes so that you can save them and find everything at the same place.
